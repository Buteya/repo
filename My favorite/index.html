Reading Books

This website is about reading books. Which happens to be my favorite thing to do, during my spare time.

Types of Books I read:

Autobiographies

Autobiographies are books that document the journey of the author through life and their life experience. In this case it is about the author and writen by the author.

Autobiographies I've read so far:

  • The story of my experiments with truth by Mahatma Gandi.
  • Long walk to freedom by Nelson Mandela.
  • The Diary of a young girl by Anne Frank.

Novels

A novel is a long prose narrative that usually describes fictional characters and events in the form of a sequential story.

Novels I've read so far:

  1. Sherlock Holmes by Authur Conan Doyle.
  2. The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho.
  3. The color purple by Alice Walker.
